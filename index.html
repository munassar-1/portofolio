Registration Steps

Choose Programme & University

In Russia, prospective students have a huge range of options: You can study for a Bachelor’s, Specialist or Master’s Degree, a doctorate, professional training or career advancement, and also learn Russian.

Prepare your Document Package

Initial Application: Copy of a document detailing your education (diploma, transcript), listing subjects studied and grades achieved (with translation into Russian) Copy of identification

Get an Invitation and Apply for a Student Visa

Invitations to Russia are issued by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Russia and the General Administration for Migration Issues of the Interior Ministry of Russia. If the applicant obtained their place at the university through a standard procedure or is a fee-paying student, the invitation will be sent by the university. You will need an original invitation to submit the visa application.
